How to Play

You need 3 published papers to graduate.

👉 Swipe Right (or Right Arrow) to submit to a journal.
👈 Swipe Left (or Left Arrow) to reject a journal.

⏰ Time is ticking: 20 seconds = 1 PhD Year. You have a max of 6 years. Taking longer than 3 years hurts your score and triggers stress music!

💰 APCs (Article Processing Charges): High impact factors cost lots of money. Minimize your costs for a high score.

⚠️ Predatory Journals: They accept instantly and are cheap, but yield 0 citations!

👻 Ghosted: Sometimes (10% chance) you wait 5 months and they just reject you because they "couldn't find reviewers".

The Reality of Publishing

This game is a satirical but grimly accurate reflection of academic publishing.

Early career researchers face immense pressure to "Publish or Perish". They navigate exorbitant Article Processing Charges (APCs) dictated by a multi-billion dollar oligopoly of publishers.

Meanwhile, review processes rely on free, uncompensated labor from peers, causing massive delays.

Initiatives like the German #ichbinhanna movement highlight the precarious working conditions and short-term contracts forced upon academics while they wait in this publication purgatory.
