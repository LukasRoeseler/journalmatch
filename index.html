The Reality of Publishing

This game is a satirical but grimly accurate reflection of academic publishing.

Early career researchers face immense pressure to "Publish or Perish". They navigate exorbitant Article Processing Charges (APCs) dictated by an oligopoly.

Meanwhile, review processes rely on free labor from peers, causing massive delays and stress, highlighted by movements like #ichbinhanna.
